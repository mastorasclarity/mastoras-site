Including

Why people come to Mástoras

Three patterns. All of them solvable — with the right conversation first.

Their offer is doing work they can't explain

Good at what they do. Can't communicate why it matters. Every pitch, every proposal, every sales conversation takes three times as long as it should — because the story isn't there yet.

They're about to commit to something that hasn't been stress-tested

A new venture. A second location. A pivot. The numbers look reasonable. The risks haven't been named. Once the money's in, walking back is expensive — getting clarity before is not.

Their funding case is real but their application sounds like everyone else's

The project is genuine. But the application reads like it was written for a committee, not a person. Funders don't fund projects — they fund narratives that make sense of projects.
